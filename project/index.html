// What is Brainwork Royale

Your real-world habits train an AI that fights for you.

Every warden owns a character with its own evolving neural net brain. You earn training generations by completing real quests — quizzes, focus sessions, fitness — and the brain compounds. Then you watch your AI replay matches against other wardens' AIs in procedural mazes.

// Character Lab

Try the rig. All 59 motions. Real physics blending.

The viewer above is the same engine the game uses. Crossfade duration, animation speed, and orbital camera are spring-damped so motion never snaps. Drag to rotate 360°, scroll or pinch to zoom, double-click to reset. Categories group locomotion, combat, and social.

▶
Spring-damped transitions
AnimationMixer crossfade with eased blend curves. No snapping when you swap from Sprint → Parry mid-motion.
0.32s DEFAULT · SCRIPTABLE
◎
Damped orbital camera
Inertia on rotate + zoom. Touch, mouse and trackpad pinch all wired. Constrained pitch so the floor never inverts.
DAMPING 0.06 · MIN/MAX ZOOM
⟂
Browser-only, GPU-light
Pure Three.js. No physics engine yet — Rapier is queued for V1 so dodges, jumps and hits collide in-world.
